# 📈 B3 Price Monitoring System

> Your automated investment assistant for the Brazilian stock market and crypto assets

## 🎯 What is B3 Price Monitoring?

**B3 Price Monitoring** is an intelligent alert platform designed for investors who want to stay on top of their investment opportunities without constantly checking market prices. Set your target price ranges once, and let the system notify you when it's time to act.

---

## 💼 Key Features

### 📊 Multi-Asset Portfolio Tracking

Monitor your entire investment portfolio in one place:

- **Brazilian Stocks** - Track B3 exchange stocks using standard ticker symbols (PETR4, VALE3, etc.)
- **Cryptocurrencies** - Keep an eye on crypto market movements
- **ETFs** - Monitor Exchange-Traded Funds

### 🎯 Smart Price Tunnel Monitoring

Create custom price ranges (called "tunnels") for each asset:

- **Upper Limit** - Set your ideal selling price
- **Lower Limit** - Set your ideal buying price
- **Custom Intervals** - Choose how frequently to check each asset
- **Multiple Tunnels** - Create different price ranges for the same asset

### 🔔 Automated Real-Time Alerts

Never miss an investment opportunity:

- **Buy Alerts** 📉 - Receive email notifications when prices fall below your lower limit
- **Sell Alerts** 📈 - Get notified when prices exceed your upper limit
- **Smart Recommendations** - Each alert includes the current price and suggested action
- **Continuous Monitoring** - System works 24/7 at your configured intervals

### 📜 Historical Price Tracking

- Records asset prices over time
- Maintains comprehensive historical data
- Track price trends for your monitored assets

### 👤 Personal Investment Dashboard

- Secure personal account for each investor
- Manage your asset portfolio
- Configure individual monitoring preferences
- Customize alert settings

## 🎬 How It Works

### Example Scenario

Imagine you're interested in **Vale S.A. (VALE3)**:

1. **Set Your Tunnel**
   - Lower limit: R$ 50.00 (buy target)
   - Upper limit: R$ 70.00 (sell target)
   - Check interval: Every 30 minutes

2. **Let the System Monitor**
   - The platform checks VALE3 prices automatically every 30 minutes

3. **Receive Alerts**
   - 📉 Stock drops to **R$ 48.00** → You receive an email: *"Time to BUY! VALE3 is below your target"*
   - 📈 Stock rises to **R$ 72.00** → You receive an email: *"Time to SELL! VALE3 exceeded your target"*
